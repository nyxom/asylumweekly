## Unexpected 'Innate' comeback, followed up by (another) Fitz crash out!

This issue led with APD Weekly's biggest headline of the period: the return of Innate and another Fitz meltdown.

## Return of the King!

APD Weekly sources report an INSANE comeback by former crybaby "Innate" after he assigned +100 points to Fitz' application for "killing the server". Wow. Couldn't have came sooner! The APD weekly staff extends a heartfelt apology and salute to Innate! Well done.

## Fitz can't take a joke!

APD Weekly obtained an EXCLUSIVE photo of Fitz as he deleted his OWN cop app. That's right, a once-in-a-lifetime event just happened: Innate, out of everyone, just made the Owner of Asylum delete their own cop app. Who saw this one coming?

## The Apocalypse...

In a desperate measure that could only be outdone by well mustached dictators, Asylum is now trying "Apocalypse Life", which is funny because "apocalypse" is how the APD Weekly would have described the state of the server post-wipe.
