      <h3>Asylum players feed for Sneed.</h3>
      <p>As we all know, the Asylum Redux game server suffered the initial week of its launch from the heinous actions and attacks of "Big Black Nigga Balls", also known as "BBNB". These criminals shamelessly cheated to win countless federal events, although the reason they needed to cheat against APD Officers is currently unknown. According to an Asylum Weekly flash poll, over 87% of APD Officers self-identify as "roleplayers", including APD Sergeant Shawn and APD Lieutenant Polar.</p>
      <p>Furthermore, after their permanent ban, BBNB members used exploits uncovered from BBNB leader "Infamous" to send people to jail en-masse and activate remote explosives. According to Asylum Weekly sources, the Grand Ayatollah of BBNB even went so far as to declare a fatwa against the Black race.</p>
      <p>Yet, attacks seemed to have died down. However, the responsible party has hinted at a new production of "Sneedflix" titled "Sneed vs. the World." Asylum Weekly will keep you all apprised.</p>
