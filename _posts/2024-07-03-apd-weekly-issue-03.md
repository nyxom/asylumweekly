## We. Are. Back!

Asylum population is better than ever with the new server wipe, despite the community's misgivings. Cop players are happier than ever with an abundance of interactions throughout the entire map, and a reduction in complicated Federal event mechanics.

## Return of the Spinner

New players may not know this, but Asylum leadership team has taken it upon themselves to unban a NUMBER of 1-2x cheaters, including the ego-maniac cheating legend, Nv Tom! Truly a sad day on Altis.

## Rogue's not rogue

APD Weekly sources have found evidence that Asylum Owner, "RoguePilot", has been spotted communing with other community leaders such as DK, Akula, and Hunter B.

## New undercover rule

New rules surrounding the infamous "Undercover" slot were released alongside wipe, most notably stopping them from patrolling with marked officers. Hopefully, this should put an end to confusion from the civilian perspective.

## Special announcement from the chief

Chief of the Altis Police Department on Asylum Life says:

> "Hello everyone, I hope you are enjoying the wipe and would like to remind everyone to double-check the new wipe specific policy updates, and moreover, make sure that you have fun. Please remember the APD Staff are here to help you with any issues! Also, don't forget to welcome returning APD Lieutenant Fried Rice back to the faction!"
